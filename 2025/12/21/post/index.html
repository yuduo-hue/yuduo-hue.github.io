Your gamepad is supported by Isaac Lab: Isaac Lab successfully enumerated it as a Carb/Omniverse gamepad device (“Microsoft X-Box 360 pad”). The reason you still get all zeros is that Se2Gamepad only updates its internal command buffer when Kit (Isaac Sim) processes input events during app updates—the callback is explicitly “when kit is updated,” and it is wired via subscribe_to_gamepad_events(...).
