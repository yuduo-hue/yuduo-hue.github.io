Hello. As the title says, I want to record my learning of Mathematical Foundation of Reinforcement Learning on the following days. There are two main reasons. Firstly, I hope that I can have a deep understanding of the tutorial, not just page through it or scroll through it. Secondly, I hope that in the future, I can be someone like Shiyu Zhao, teaching MFRL to others, for examples my parents. From my point of view, learing and teaching are closely connected. You can never know if you master sth until you make sb master it.
